Few words about climate change and global warming

by Susanne Smith
Aug 31.

What is global warming?

As we know, global warming describes the current rise in the average temperature of Earth's air and oceans. So it relates closely to climate change, because the result is the climate change. The weather has become very extreme. We have terrible storms and heat waves. But whether is there a solution to this problem at all? I want to share my thoughts on this now with you in this little blog.

In my opinion, climate change would be reversible, if people would deal with it much more. I mean that each person should be do something little every single day. Perhaps, select the rubbish, or don't buy more plastic, but if we think about it, we can do many more little things in our personal life. We just need to start right now.

Naturally, it's also our personal decision, so we have to make a decision yet today. If we wait for a long time it can be possible that it will be late. I have tried to examine why people don't want to step. What is the most important in their life and why? I have given these questions to many people, from my friends to unfamiliar people around me. I have tried to find out why is it so hard to do anything in this subject? The answer has been in the most cases that "I don't believe in the climate change." or "I don't have time for doing anything. I am very busy every day."

If you get these same questions what is your answer for them? Please share your opinion about this article, I would love to get to know your personal viewpoint. What do you think, will we able to change our thinking? Can planting trees solve this problem? Do you have time for changings or you are also very busy as others? What is the most important in your life?

According to me, we still can do many things to change the situation. First of all, we can protect our surroundings from too much waste, then we can plant trees as a volunteer. If we don't kill too many animals and we reinstall them back we can rescue the animal world from extinction. But the most important is the time. We need to act right now! We cannot wait more! If we do something yet today, we will have a beautiful future in this place, but if don't, we will extinction, as other species and we can tell then: That was our responibility. Is it sure that we really want this?

I don't want to live a place like seeing below. But it is imaginable that we will have a habitat like this in the future. We will not have enough drinking water, and food, and the weather will be too hot and dry. We will not able to live in this kind of world. That's why, the question is very essential: Why don't we do something? The Earth is yours too.
